Such insolence! I tire of placating fools trying to entice them! As if imprisonment is not enough, I must also play games with such as you? Such indignity that I have been trapped here since that fool Durlag disappeared, and now I have to deal with your rejection as well?! Durlag may have taken my wings and cursed me to remain here, but I've still a few powers his traps couldn't strip! If I cannot leave, neither will you!
